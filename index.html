About Me

Hi Internet!

I am an Artist & Graphic Designer in New York City. Nothing brings me more joy than losing myself in work that excites me and triumphantly coming out on the other side.

I studied Studio Arts and Film @ Bard College. I have experience in an array of different mediums, including: web design, mobile UI, video production & editing, photography, illustration, print, and videogame design.
